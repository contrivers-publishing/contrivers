Have something to say?

Contrivers' Review doesn't publish comments, but we do publish Responses.

A good response opens up a conversation about a piece of writing. It might question a particular framing, research perspective, or normative conclusion. It might draw on anecdotal personal experience or scholarly knowledge. Crucially, it engages the original essay or review in a honest and productive way.

If you have something to say, email us at editors@contrivers.org.
